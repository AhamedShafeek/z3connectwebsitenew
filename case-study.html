Retail

Multi-Store Retail POS System

Mobile billing system with real-time inventory sync reducing daily admin work from 3 hours to 20 minutes.

Client Context

A retail chain with 5 locations across Tamil Nadu selling home appliances and electronics. Team of 12 staff members across stores. Monthly revenue of ₹40-50 lakhs.

Owner spent 2-3 hours daily consolidating sales data from handwritten bills and Excel sheets. No real-time inventory visibility led to frequent stockouts and overstocking.

The Problem

Manual Billing Process

Handwritten bills leading to calculation errors, slow checkout times, and no digital records for analysis.

No Inventory Visibility

Stock levels tracked manually in notebooks. No way to check stock across locations. Frequent emergency transfers between stores.

Time-Consuming Reporting

Owner spent 3 hours every evening consolidating data from 5 stores manually in Excel to understand daily performance.

Our Solution

We built a custom mobile POS system using React Native, Node.js backend, and real-time Firebase sync. Deployed across all 5 locations with offline-first architecture.

Mobile POS

Fast billing with barcode scanning, product search, automatic tax calculation, and instant digital invoices.

Real-Time Inventory

Live stock levels across all locations. Auto-alerts for low stock. Inter-store transfer tracking.

Auto Reporting

Daily sales reports generated automatically. Store-wise performance. Product-wise analytics. Expense tracking.

Offline Mode

Works without internet. Syncs when connection restored. Zero downtime during network issues.

Implementation

Week 1: Requirements gathering and workflow mapping. Tested pilot version at one store with 2 staff members.

Week 2: Refined based on feedback. Added barcode scanner support. Built product catalog migration tool.

Week 3: Full rollout across all 5 locations. On-site training for all staff. Live support for first week.

Ongoing: Weekly check-ins. Feature updates based on usage patterns. System monitoring and optimization.

Results & Impact

3hrs → 20min
Daily Admin Time
60%
Faster Checkouts
Zero
Stock Errors

Owner now gets daily performance reports automatically on WhatsApp. Real-time visibility into which products are selling, which stores are performing, and exact stock levels.

Staff spend zero time on manual calculations. Checkout is faster. Customers get professional digital invoices. No more stockouts or emergency transfers.

Business operates like a tech company — with data, analytics, and automation — while staying affordable for MSME scale.
