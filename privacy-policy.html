Privacy Policy

Z3Connect Technologies

Last updated: 15/12/2024

Z3Connect Technologies ("Z3Connect", "we", "our", "us") operates software products and services including Content Flow, a content creation and scheduling platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently and responsibly.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our website and products.

1

Information We Collect

a) Information you provide

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Company name
  • Account login details
  • Content you create or schedule using our tools

b) Social media account information (with your consent)

When you connect third-party platforms such as LinkedIn, Facebook, or Instagram, we may access:

  • Basic profile information
  • Account or Page IDs
  • Posting permissions required to publish content

We do not access passwords for any third-party platform.

c) Technical information

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Log data related to system usage
2

How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly to:

  • Provide and operate our services
  • Publish content to connected social media accounts only when explicitly authorized
  • Schedule and manage posts
  • Improve platform reliability and performance
  • Communicate service-related updates

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data
  • Scrape private information
  • Perform automated engagement (likes, follows, comments)
  • Access data beyond approved permissions
3

Third-Party Platform Usage

Z3Connect integrates with official APIs provided by:

  • LinkedIn
  • Meta Platforms (Facebook & Instagram)
  • Other supported social platforms

Data accessed through these APIs is:

  • Used only for the intended functionality
  • Stored securely
  • Never shared with unauthorized parties

Our use of data complies fully with each platform's developer policies.

4

Data Storage & Security

We take security seriously and implement:

  • Encrypted data storage
  • Secure access controls
  • Restricted internal access
  • Regular system monitoring

Access tokens and credentials are stored securely and refreshed according to platform guidelines.

5

Data Retention

We retain user data only for as long as:

  • Your account remains active
  • It is required to provide our services
  • Legal or regulatory obligations apply

You may request data deletion at any time.

6

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Disconnect social media accounts
  • Request deletion of your data

Requests can be made by contacting us at the email below.

7

Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain user sessions
  • Improve user experience
  • Analyze platform usage

You may disable cookies through your browser settings.

8

Children's Privacy

Z3Connect services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18.

We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10

Contact Information

For any privacy-related questions or requests:
